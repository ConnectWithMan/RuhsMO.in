Frequently Asked Questions

Candidates possessing an MBBS degree from a university recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and registered with the Rajasthan Medical Council are eligible to apply.
Generally, candidates must be between 22 and 45 years of age. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per Rajasthan government norms.
The Medical Officer post falls under Pay Matrix Level-14. Initially, during the probation period, a fixed remuneration is given, post which full allowances are provided.
The exam usually consists of 100 objective type (MCQ) questions based on the standard MBBS curriculum. Total duration is typically 2 hours.
The selection is based on the marks obtained in the online/offline written examination, followed by document verification. There is no interview round.
