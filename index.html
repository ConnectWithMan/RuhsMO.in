About Rajasthan MO Recruitment 2026

The Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS) conducts the Medical Officer (MO) direct recruitment examination to fill vacancies across various government medical institutions in Rajasthan. This is a highly sought-after opportunity for medical professionals looking to serve in the state healthcare sector.

Candidates selected as Medical Officers will be placed under the standard pay scale as determined by the Rajasthan Government. The selection process typically involves a Off-line Test, followed by rigorous document verification to ensure all eligibility and NMC registration requirements are meticulously met.

Stay tuned to this portal for real-time updates regarding the Rajasthan MO Notification 2026, application deadlines, syllabus PDF downloads, and admit card release dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Candidates must possess an MBBS degree from a university recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and must be registered with the Rajasthan Medical Council.

You can apply online through the official RUHS recruitment portal once the active link is provided in the "Important Dates" or "Updates" section.

Please refer to the latest official syllabus and examination pattern booklet in the Quick Links section for confirmation regarding negative marking.

Generally, the age limit is between 22 and 45 years. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per Rajasthan government rules.

The selection process usually consists of a computer-based written examination followed by document verification and the generation of a final merit list.

The application fee varies by category (UR, OBC, SC, ST, etc.). Please check the official detailed notification for the exact fee structure once released.
