Learn how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information while you use our portal.

Welcome to Rajasthan MO. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

By accessing or using our portal, you signify your understanding and agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Log Data: Information your browser automatically sends whenever you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. We may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Increase the efficiency and operation of the Site.
