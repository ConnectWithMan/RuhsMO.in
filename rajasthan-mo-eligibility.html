RUHS MO Eligibility Criteria 2026

Comprehensive breakdown of the educational qualifications, age limit, and registration requirements for the upcoming Rajasthan University of Health Sciences Medical Officer Examination.

Educational Qualification

To be eligible for the RUHS Medical Officer post, candidates must possess the following basic educational qualifications:

  • MBBS Degree: A recognized MBBS degree under the National Medical Commission (NMC) / Medical Council of India (MCI) Act.
  • Internship: Completion of the mandatory one-year compulsory rotating internship.
  • RMC Registration: Permanent or provisional registration with the Rajasthan Medical Council (RMC) is strictly required.

Rajasthan Domicile & Reservation Policies

The RUHS Medical Officer recruitment strictly follows the reservation policies mandated by the Government of Rajasthan. Important points regarding reservations include:

  • Domicile Requirement: The benefits of category-based reservations (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS) and corresponding age relaxations are exclusively available to the bona fide residents (domiciles) of Rajasthan.
  • Other State Candidates: Candidates from outside Rajasthan are eligible to apply if they meet the core qualifications. However, they will be considered as Unreserved (General) category candidates, irrespective of their original category in their home state.
  • Female Reservation: Horizontal reservation is applicable for female candidates as per the prevailing state government norms.
  • Valid Documentation: Candidates claiming reservation benefits must possess valid and recently issued caste, category, and domicile certificates from the competent authorities of Rajasthan.
