Factors Affecting the Cutoff

The official cutoff marks for the Rajasthan Medical Officer recruitment can vary each year depending on several key factors:

  • Number of Vacancies: Higher number of vacancies usually leads to a lower cutoff and vice versa.
  • Difficulty Level of the Exam: If the paper is tough, the overall cutoff tends to decrease.
  • Number of Applicants: An increase in the number of candidates appearing for the exam increases the competition and the cutoff marks.
  • Reservation Norms: Category-wise reservations set by the Rajasthan Government also play a major role in determining individual category cutoffs.
