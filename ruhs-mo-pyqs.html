Why Solve RUHS MO Previous Year Papers?

Previous year papers are the single best resource to understand exactly how RUHS frames its Medical Officer exam. Here's what they help with:

Real Exam Pattern

Understand the actual number of questions, marking scheme, negative marking, and sectional distribution used by RUHS.

Repeated Topics

Identify subjects and topics from Medicine, Surgery, Gynae, Pediatrics, and PSM that recur across multiple years.

Time Management

Practicing full-length previous papers under timed conditions builds speed and exam-day stamina.

Self-Assessment

Compare your score against the answer key to identify weak subjects and plan focused revision.
