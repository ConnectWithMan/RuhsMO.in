Rajasthan MO Salary Structure 2026

The salary for a Medical Officer in Rajasthan is highly lucrative and is governed by the 7th Pay Commission rules of the State Government. Below is the detailed salary structure:

Component Details
Pay Matrix Level Level - 14 (L-14)
Grade Pay ₹ 5400/-
Basic Pay ₹ 56,100/- (upon completion of probation)
Pay Band PB-3 (₹ 15600 - 39100)

Salary During Probation Period

Like all Rajasthan Government jobs, newly recruited Medical Officers are placed on a probation period. During this time, they receive a fixed monthly remuneration.

  • Probation Duration: Typically 1 year for Medical Officers.
  • Fixed Remuneration: Approximately ₹ 56,100/- per month during the probation period.
  • Allowances during Probation: No other allowances (like DA, HRA) are paid during the probation period, except for standard deductions.

Allowances & Perks (After Probation)

Upon successful completion of the probation period, Medical Officers are entitled to various allowances as per Rajasthan Government norms:

  • Dearness Allowance (DA): Revised periodically by the state government based on inflation.
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA): Depends on the posting location (typically 9% or 18% of basic pay in Rajasthan).
  • Non-Practicing Allowance (NPA): Granted to doctors who do not engage in private practice, usually 20% of the basic pay.
  • Other Perks: Medical facilities, pension benefits (NPS/OPS as applicable), and earned leaves.
