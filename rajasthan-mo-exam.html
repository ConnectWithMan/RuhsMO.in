Rajasthan MO Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the exam pattern is crucial for effective preparation. The Rajasthan MO examination generally follows this structure:

  • Mode of Examination: Offline Test.
  • Type of Questions: Objective Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
  • Total Questions: 100 Questions.
  • Total Marks: 100 Marks (1 mark for each correct answer).
  • Duration: The exam is typically conducted for a duration of 2 hours (120 minutes).
